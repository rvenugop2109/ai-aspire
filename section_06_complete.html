Assessment
Low Stakes, High Frequency — The Assessment Architecture
EVIDENCE
The Governing Idea

A record of
capability —
not a judgement.

Every assessment in this programme is designed to surface what students can do — not catch them out. Quizzes are short and frequent. Portfolios show growth over time. The live showcase is the moment everything is demonstrated publicly.

📈
Low stakes, high frequency
3 quizzes per year — 15–20 minutes each. No grade anxiety. Just regular calibration.
📁
Portfolio carries equal weight
Artefacts — journals, prompt portfolios, research reports, build logs — are assessed alongside written tests.
🎤
Live defence from Grade 7
Grade 7 AI Report · Grade 8 Mini-Project panel · Grade 9 Capstone panel with external guest.

Credentialing
The Credential Pathway — Four Badges, One Certificate
EARNED
The Credential Model

One badge
per year.
One certificate
to close it.

Students earn a stackable Campuslogix AI Aspire badge at the end of each grade — evidence that year's capabilities are in place. All four badges together, plus the Capstone, earn the AI Foundation Certificate.

🎓
Campuslogix AI Foundation Certificate
Awarded on completion of all four grades and successful presentation of the Grade 9 Capstone to a live panel. The credential is evidence of four years of compounded, demonstrated capability — not a course completion.

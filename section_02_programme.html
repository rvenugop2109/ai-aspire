The Journey
Four Years. Four Chapters. One Direction.
How It Compounds

Each year
earns the
next one.

The programme is not four standalone courses. It is one four-year journey — each grade requires mastery of the previous. A student who starts in Grade 6 arrives at Grade 9 with compounded capability that cannot be rushed or replicated in a single year.

Grade 6 — foundations in reasoning and AI literacy
Grade 7 — deep understanding and structured prompting
Grade 8 — real-world application and vibe coding
Grade 9 — critical evaluation and capstone creation

The Outcome
What a Completed Student Looks Like
G9
Four years in. What changes.

Capabilities most
adults in the
workforce
don't yet have.

A student who finishes all four grades holds something that cannot be faked in an interview or an exam — demonstrated, practised, panel-defended capability.

Pillar 1
Solve complex problems by decomposing them into structured steps — and explain their reasoning, not just their answer.
Pillar 2
Evaluate any AI claim — from a news article, a product pitch, or a policy brief — for accuracy, bias, and intent.
Pillar 3
Design and deploy system prompts, chain prompts, and a personal Prompt Toolkit — across every tool they use.
Pillar 4
Build a working multi-modal AI project — vibe-coded app, AI voice, AI video — and present it to a live panel.
Pillar 5
Conduct a full ethical review of any AI product brief — identifying harms, accountability gaps, and safeguards.
